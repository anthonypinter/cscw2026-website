REGISTRATION

CSCW 2026 will be held in Salt Lake City, Utah, USA, October 10-14, 2026.
The early bird registration deadline is August 11, 2026. All dates are Anywhere on Earth (AoE) time. To learn more about online attendance this year, please read this blog post.
Visit this link to register: https://cvent.me/NyyexR
Note: Workshop registration is now open.
In line with SIGCHI’s commitment to equity and global inclusivity, CSCW offers deeply discounted rates for registrations from countries designated by the ACM as “economically developing.” Countries in Category H receive a 40% discount, and those in Category I receive a 70% discount. These discounts are applied at the end of the registration process based on the country in which the registrant resides.
