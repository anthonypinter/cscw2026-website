SPONSORSHIP

The ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing (CSCW) is the premier venue for human-centered research in the design, use, and evaluation of technologies that support or affect social, cooperative, and collaborative practices in groups, organizations, communities, and networks. Bringing together top researchers and practitioners, CSCW 2026 will explore topics across sociotechnical domains of work, home, education, healthcare, the arts, design, entertainment, and ethics, including social computing and social media, crowdsourcing, and technologies for co-located or remote collaboration, communication, education, work articulation, coordination, awareness, and information sharing.
CSCW 2026 will be held in Salt Lake City, Utah, USA, October 10-14, 2026.
As a business with expertise and experience with themes related to CSCW, we invite you to become a financial sponsor of CSCW 2026. Your sponsorship confers three main benefits to CSCW attendees and your company. First, your sponsorship will help keep conference costs accessible for students and enhance the conference experience for all attendees worldwide. Second, as a sponsor, you will have the opportunity to showcase your organization’s work and recruit candidates with skills in the design, engineering, and other aspects of CSCW. Third, as a sponsor, you will have the opportunity to get involved and influence the conference events and the international CSCW community as a whole.
For further details on the conference: https://cscw.acm.org/2026/

SPONSORS

Sponsoring ACM CSCW is an ideal way to demonstrate your organization’s interest in and commitment to the field of collaborative software and technology, and to publicize this support to many leaders in the field. Corporate support is typically used to permit a greater number of students to attend CSCW at reduced fees without increasing the general registration cost, and to allow student volunteers to receive complimentary registration. Corporate support can also be targeted to a particular event or activity during the conference.
Your support is very important to the CSCW 2026 conference, and the conference committee ensures that these contributions are well recognized. The conference offers six corporate support levels: Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze, Friend, and Supporter. We list the benefits and costs below. We can also create individually tailored sponsorship packages for your organization according to your specific interests. For example, we could include your company’s logo on the conference badge lanyards carried by all conference participants, convert any included sponsor registrations to (named) free registrations to be given to student volunteers or other students in need, name a social event after your company, set up a table at the main conference area throughout the conference days, and so on.
If you are interested in supporting CSCW 2026, please contact our Industry Liaison chairs at industry2026@cscw.acm.org or General Chairs at chairs2026@cscw.acm.org.
